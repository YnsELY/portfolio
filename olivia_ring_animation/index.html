Yoovi

Yoovi is an AI-powered educational platform for French middle and high school students (grades 6 through Terminale), built in direct collaboration with the French Ministry of Education and already being deployed in schools across France.

- 24/7 intelligent tutoring assistant built around subject-specific Virtual Rooms.
- Students can chat with AI, generate flashcards, quizzes and revision sheets, take notes, and complete exercises by subject and chapter.
- Multimodal input: text, audio, images, and PDFs, plus real-time screen sharing with live AI assistance and voice calls with the AI.
- Includes a parent monitoring dashboard and a career guidance module.
- Freemium model: free access for all students, with advanced features in 3 paid tiers (monthly or annual).
- Positioned as an ethics-first learning companion, not a cheating tool, to democratize quality academic support and reduce educational inequality across France.

Child's Kitchen

Child's Kitchen is a school catering platform that connects parents, meal suppliers, and schools in one synchronized system for child menu management.

Parents can reserve and pay for their child's weekly menus directly in the app. Suppliers get a clear daily production view of all meals to prepare, and schools can identify which menu must be distributed to each student. The platform provides dedicated access for parents, suppliers, and schools to automate the full workflow end-to-end, and it is deployed for schools in Morocco.

Created By:

Yanis El Yassiri

Date:

2023

Client:

Chicken Maestro

Categories:

Restaurant Ordering, White-Label Delivery, Courier Operations
